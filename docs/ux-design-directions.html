EngageNinja – Design Directions

Direction A · Clarity & Uplift

Minimal, high-contrast surfaces that spotlight the hero loop: Send → Resend → Prove uplift. Live delivery/read ticks and uplift snapshots are always visible.

Live status chipsOne-click resendInline reasons
  • Dashboard hero: uplift card, resend-ready card, WA health.
  • Campaign row: statuses, resend CTA, uplift delta in-line.
  • Banners for impersonation/support with clear exit.
Direction B · Agency Ops

Ops-first dashboard with tenant switcher, ROI per tenant, and checklists for onboarding. More data-dense tables and role clarity cues.

Tenant switcherROI per tenantChecklist home
  • Global bar: tenant switcher + impersonation banner.
  • Per-tenant cards: sends, reads, resend usage, trends.
  • Checklist panel driving Connect → Import → Send → Resend.
Direction C · Guided Launch

Wizard-like guidance with inline mapping and preflight safety rails. Friendly microcopy emphasizes “you’re safe to send”.

Preflight checklistInline mappingGuided resend
  • 3-step linear flow with visible progress: Audience → Template/Variables → Review & Send.
  • Preflight cards showing approvals/consent/recipients; send button states.
  • Resend card with countdown and ready state.
